Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version Endur works offline and stores your data locally on your device by default. If you create an account, only your email address and your challenge data are stored on our servers — nothing else. No ads. No tracking. No selling data. Ever.

1. Who we are

Endur is a endurance & expedition challenge tracker available at merry-selkie-4ae2f1.netlify.app. Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@endur.app.

2. Data we collect

Without an account (offline mode)

All data — your challenges, habits, streaks, body measurements, badges — is stored only in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device. We collect nothing.

With a free account (cloud sync)

When you create an account we store:

  • Your email address — used to identify your account
  • A hashed password — your actual password is never stored; only a one-way SHA-256 hash
  • Your app state — challenges, habits, streaks, badges and body data, so it can be restored on a new device

We do not collect your name, location, device identifiers, IP address, or any behavioural analytics.

3. How data is stored

Account data is stored using Supabase, a managed PostgreSQL database service. Supabase's infrastructure is hosted in the United States. By creating an account you acknowledge that your data will be processed in the US.

Supabase's own privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

4. Cookies & tracking

Endur uses no cookies, no analytics scripts, no advertising pixels, and no third-party tracking of any kind. The only browser storage used is localStorage for app state and session tokens.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party. The only sub-processor is Netlify (hosting and storage), as described in section 3.

6. Your rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request that your account and all associated data be permanently deleted
  • Portability — export your data at any time using the Export backup button in Settings
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@endur.app. We will respond within 30 days.

You can also delete your own data at any time: use the Export/Restore section in Settings, or simply clear your browser storage. If you have an account and want it permanently removed, email us.

7. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, all data is removed within 7 days. Offline-only data is never on our servers and can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser storage.

8. Children

Endur is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@endur.app
